Our Mission

At Le Santos School, our mission is to provide a holistic education that nurtures the intellectual, emotional, and social growth of our students.We strive to create a supportive community that encourages creativity, critical thinking, and collaboration, preparing our students to make a positive impact in the world.

Our Vision

To be a premier educational institution that fosters a culture of excellence, innovation, and inclusivity, empowering students to become compassionate leaders and lifelong learners.

Our Motto

"Learn with Passion, Lead with Purpose"

Our Core Values

  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in all endeavors.
  • Inclusivity: Embracing diversity and fostering a sense of belonging among all members of the school community.
  • Creativity: Encouraging innovation and imagination in learning and problem-solving.
  • Compassion: Cultivating empathy and kindness towards others.
  • Integrity: Upholding honesty, responsibility, and moral principles in all actions.

Le Santos School

Le Santos School was founded in 1995 by visionary educators aiming to provide quality education with a focus on community service and personal development. Named after Epifanio de los Santos, it honored his contributions to education and culture. By 2005, the school expanded its facilities, introducing STEM and arts programs. This growth led to recognition for innovative teaching methods and community projects.In 2010, the school received the "Best Community Service Award" for environmental conservation and literacy initiatives.Today, Le Santos School integrates technology into its curriculum and plans international partnerships for student exchanges. Core values include excellence, community, innovation, and integrity. The school continues to evolve, fostering a supportive environment that prepares students for a global future
